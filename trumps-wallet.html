About This Data

Source: All data processed directly from Donald Trump's OGE Form 278 filings. Original files obtained by Hunter Index (nonprofit, EIN 33-3157569) or partners. Any use must be attributed to Hunter Index with a link to hunterindex.org.

Annual forms cover the preceding calendar year. Candidate forms cover ~12 months prior to filing. The 2023 annual form was not processed due to an OGE lawsuit note. Values are OGE-reported ranges; medians are calculated estimates. Not yet processed: 2017, 2018, 2021 termination report, 2026 transaction report (filed 2/26/2026). 2026 annual expected by August 13, 2026.
